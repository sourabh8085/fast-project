Key Aspects of Data:
Types: Data is categorized into quantitative (numerical/measurable, e.g., temperature, cost) and qualitative (descriptive, e.g., text, colors).
Purpose: Raw data is processed into information to uncover trends, improve business outcomes, and inform decisions.
Computing: In digital contexts, data is stored in binary formats and can be classified by its state: at rest, in transit, or in use.
